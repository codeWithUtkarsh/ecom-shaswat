# Questions to Ask Vyapaar Global — Complete Website Checklist

---

## 1. BUSINESS & BRANDING

- [ ] **Company registration name** — Is it "Vyapaar Global Ltd"? What's the exact legal entity name for invoices/footer?
- [ ] **Logo** — Do you have a logo file (SVG/PNG)? The current "V" icon is a placeholder.
- [ ] **Brand colours** — Are you happy with the current dark theme + neon green, or do you have brand guidelines?
- [ ] **Tagline confirmation** — "Agri-Commerce Reimagined" and "From farm to home" — are these final?
- [ ] **Domain name** — What domain will this run on? (e.g., vyapaarglobal.com)

---

## 2. PRODUCT CATALOGUE

- [ ] **Product data source** — Where does your real product data come from? CSV/spreadsheet? ERP system? Manual entry?
- [ ] **How many products** will you have at launch? 50? 500? 5000+?
- [ ] **Product images** — Do you have professional product photography, or do you need placeholder/stock images?
- [ ] **Categories** — Are the current 10 categories (Dairy, Organic, Fruits, Spices, Snacks, Vegetables, Grains, Pulses, Oils, Beverages) correct? Any to add/remove?
- [ ] **Product variants** — Do products have variants? (e.g., 1kg vs 5kg vs 25kg bags)
- [ ] **Unit pricing** — Do you price per unit, per kg, per case, per pallet?
- [ ] **Minimum order quantity (MOQ)** — Is there a minimum order per product for B2B?
- [ ] **Bulk pricing tiers** — Do prices change based on quantity? (e.g., 1-10 units = £5, 11-50 = £4.50, 50+ = £4)
- [ ] **Product availability** — Should stock levels show on the site? Or is everything "available on request"?
- [ ] **Seasonal products** — Do some products come and go seasonally?

---

## 3. CUSTOMERS & ACCOUNTS

- [ ] **Who are your customers?** — UK retailers only? Restaurants? Wholesalers? Individual consumers too?
- [ ] **Account approval** — Can anyone sign up and order, or do new accounts need manual approval/verification first?
- [ ] **Business verification** — Do you need to verify customers are legitimate businesses? (Company number, VAT number, trade license?)
- [ ] **Customer tiers** — Do different customers get different pricing? (e.g., Gold/Silver/Bronze retailers)
- [ ] **Credit terms** — Do you offer Net 30/60 payment terms, or is everything pay-on-order?
- [ ] **Multiple delivery addresses** — Can one account have multiple delivery locations (e.g., a chain with 5 stores)?

---

## 4. ORDERING & CHECKOUT

- [ ] **Payment methods** — What payment methods do you want to accept?
  - Credit/Debit cards (Stripe?)
  - Bank transfer / BACS
  - PayPal
  - Credit terms / invoicing
  - Purchase orders?
- [ ] **Currency** — GBP only, or do you also deal in USD/EUR?
- [ ] **VAT handling** — Are all prices shown ex-VAT or inc-VAT? Standard 20% on everything, or are some products zero-rated?
- [ ] **Invoice generation** — Do you need automatic PDF invoices? What details must appear on them?
- [ ] **Order confirmation** — What should happen when an order is placed? Email to customer? Email to your team? SMS?
- [ ] **Reorder feature** — Should customers be able to quickly reorder previous orders?
- [ ] **Quote/RFQ system** — For large orders, should customers be able to request a custom quote instead of buying directly?

---

## 5. DELIVERY & LOGISTICS

- [ ] **Delivery areas** — UK-wide? Specific regions only? Do you deliver to Scotland/NI/Channel Islands?
- [ ] **Delivery charges** — Flat rate? Weight-based? Free above a certain order value (currently set to £500)?
- [ ] **Delivery timeframes** — Same-day? Next-day? 2-3 business days? Does it vary by location?
- [ ] **Delivery slots** — Can customers choose a preferred delivery date/time?
- [ ] **Tracking** — Do you provide tracking numbers? Which courier service? (DPD, Royal Mail, own fleet?)
- [ ] **Cold chain** — Do perishable items need temperature-controlled delivery? How is this handled?
- [ ] **Click & collect** — Is warehouse pickup an option?

---

## 6. CONTENT PAGES (Currently Missing)

- [ ] **About Us** — Company story, mission, team. Do you have this copy written?
- [ ] **Contact page** — Phone, email, contact form, physical address, Google Maps embed?
- [ ] **Terms & Conditions** — Do you have a solicitor drafting these, or do you need template T&Cs?
- [ ] **Privacy Policy** — Required by UK GDPR. Do you have this drafted?
- [ ] **Cookie Policy** — Do you need a cookie consent banner?
- [ ] **Returns & Refunds Policy** — What's your policy on damaged goods, wrong items, returns?
- [ ] **Delivery Information page** — Detailed shipping info, areas covered, timeframes
- [ ] **FAQ page** — Common questions from retailers
- [ ] **Blog/News** — Do you want a blog section for supply chain updates, new product announcements?

---

## 7. SUPPLIER / VENDOR SIDE

- [ ] **Supplier profiles** — Should suppliers (Punjab Agro, FarmLink Direct, etc.) have their own pages?
- [ ] **Supplier portal** — Do suppliers need login access to manage their own products/inventory?
- [ ] **Become a supplier** — Should there be an application form for new suppliers?
- [ ] **Supplier certifications** — Do you display certifications (organic, fair trade, FSSAI, etc.)?

---

## 8. ADMIN & OPERATIONS

- [ ] **Admin dashboard** — Do you need a backend panel to:
  - Manage products (add/edit/delete)
  - View and process orders
  - Manage customers
  - View sales reports/analytics
  - Manage inventory levels
- [ ] **Who manages the site?** — Technical team? Or non-technical staff who need a simple CMS?
- [ ] **Order workflow** — What's the order lifecycle? (Pending → Confirmed → Packed → Shipped → Delivered?)
- [ ] **Notification preferences** — When should your team get alerted? (New order, low stock, new customer signup?)
- [ ] **Reporting** — What reports do you need? (Daily sales, top products, customer spend, etc.)

---

## 9. MARKETING & SEO

- [ ] **Google Analytics** — Do you have a GA4 tracking ID?
- [ ] **Facebook Pixel / Meta** — Do you run social ads?
- [ ] **SEO requirements** — Do you have target keywords? Do product pages need structured data (schema.org)?
- [ ] **Email marketing** — Do you use Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or similar? Need newsletter integration?
- [ ] **Discount codes / Coupons** — Do you want a promo code system? (e.g., WELCOME10 for 10% off first order)
- [ ] **Referral program** — Do you want retailers to refer other retailers?
- [ ] **Social media links** — What are your actual social media URLs? (LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, WhatsApp)

---

## 10. TECHNICAL & HOSTING

- [ ] **Hosting** — Where should this be deployed? (Vercel, AWS, own server?)
- [ ] **Domain & DNS** — Who manages your domain? Do you need help setting up DNS?
- [ ] **SSL certificate** — Handled by hosting provider, or do you have one?
- [ ] **Email service** — What email provider for transactional emails? (SendGrid, AWS SES, Postmark?)
- [ ] **Supabase plan** — Free tier has limits. For production, you'll need a paid plan. Approved?
- [ ] **Payment gateway account** — Do you have a Stripe/PayPal business account set up?
- [ ] **Uptime requirements** — Any SLA expectations? 99.9% uptime?
- [ ] **Backup strategy** — How often should data be backed up?

---

## 11. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE

- [ ] **Company registration** — Companies House number for footer/invoices
- [ ] **VAT registration number** — For invoices and checkout
- [ ] **Food safety certifications** — SALSA, BRC, HACCP — do these need displaying?
- [ ] **Import licenses** — Any certifications to show for imported goods?
- [ ] **Data protection** — ICO registration number (UK GDPR requirement)
- [ ] **Allergen information** — Do products need allergen labelling on the website?

---

## 12. LAUNCH PRIORITIES

Ask the owner to rank these in order of priority:

1. **Phase 1 (Must-have for launch):**
   - [ ] Real product data & images
   - [ ] Working checkout with payment
   - [ ] Order confirmation emails
   - [ ] Basic account pages (profile, order history)
   - [ ] Legal pages (T&Cs, Privacy, Returns)
   - [ ] Contact page
   - [ ] Deployment to production domain

2. **Phase 2 (Soon after launch):**
   - [ ] Admin dashboard for order/product management
   - [ ] Bulk ordering & tiered pricing
   - [ ] Invoice generation
   - [ ] Search & filtering
   - [ ] Analytics integration

3. **Phase 3 (Growth features):**
   - [ ] Supplier portal
   - [ ] Customer tiers & credit terms
   - [ ] Reorder functionality
   - [ ] Blog / content pages
   - [ ] Email marketing integration
   - [ ] Referral program
